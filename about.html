ABOUT ME




A self-motivated and responsible designer that tackles each and every project with a positive attitude. I am proficient with Adobe Creative Suites (Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign) and can work on both Macintosh and PC platforms. Through my years in print production, I gained a great amount of experience with Heidelberg Cockpit and Prinect Signa softwares. Though I’ve worked in graphic design and print production for many years, I have become very much intrigued to learn web and interactive design. I believe this would help me achieve my goal to become the most valuable designer that I can become. In the future I hope to be able to provide this skill to a company that believes in it’s employee as much as he believes in them.
